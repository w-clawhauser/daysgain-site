## About Fintify

Fintify helps users track:

- Portfolio value
- Holdings
- Buy and sell transactions
- Deposits and withdrawals
- Dividends
- Estimated passive income
- Multiple accounts
- Multiple markets and currencies

Fintify is designed for personal tracking and informational purposes only.

It does not provide financial advice, investment recommendations, brokerage services, tax advice, or legal advice.

## Data and Privacy

Fintify is designed as a local-first app.

Your portfolio records are stored on your device unless you choose to export, share, or otherwise provide them outside the app.
