# About Fintify

Fintify is a personal portfolio and passive income tracking app designed for self-directed investors who want a clearer view of their financial picture.

Fintify helps you see through scattered accounts, markets, holdings, returns, and dividend income in one clear view.

## Why Fintify Exists

Fintify was built from a real personal need: a better way to understand investments spread across multiple accounts, brokerages, markets, currencies, and holdings.

For Canadian investors, records can quickly become fragmented across TFSA, RRSP, non-registered accounts, different brokerages, Canadian and US securities, ETFs, dividend stocks, cash activities, and deposit or withdrawal history.

For registered accounts such as TFSA and RRSP, keeping a clear personal record of deposits and withdrawals can also be helpful when reviewing contribution history.

Fintify brings these pieces together into one clear view.

## What Fintify Helps Track

Fintify helps you track:

- Portfolio value
- Holdings
- Buy and sell transactions
- Deposits and withdrawals
- Account breakdowns
- Owner-level summaries
- Dividend income
- Estimated passive income
- Dividend yield
- Yield on cost
- Multiple accounts
- Multiple brokerages
- Multiple markets and currencies
- Dividend and stock split events when available from market data providers

## Who Fintify Is For

Fintify is designed for people who manage their own investment records and want better visibility across accounts and markets.

It may be useful if you:

- Hold investments across multiple accounts
- Track TFSA, RRSP, and non-registered investment accounts
- Use more than one brokerage
- Invest in Canadian and US stocks or ETFs
- Care about dividend income and passive income visibility
- Want to keep personal records of deposits and withdrawals
- Prefer CSV import and export instead of brokerage login
- Want a local-first portfolio tracking tool

## Key Features

### Wealth Lens Dashboard

See portfolio value, cost, return, day’s gain/loss, holdings, and account breakdowns in one place.

### Passive Income Clarity

Track dividend income, estimated future payments, dividend yield, yield on cost, and average monthly passive income.

### Multi-Account Organization

Organize records by owner, account type, institution, and account.

### Multi-Market Support

Track US, Canadian, and China A-share securities, including stocks and ETFs.

### Transaction and Cash Activity Records

Record buys, sells, deposits, and withdrawals.

### TFSA, RRSP, and Non-Registered Account Tracking

Keep personal records of registered and non-registered investment accounts, including deposits and withdrawals.

### CSV Import and Export

Import transaction history, export backups, and keep your data portable.

### Dividend and Stock Event History

View dividend and stock split events when available from market data providers.

### Local-First Design

Fintify does not require a brokerage login.

Your portfolio records are stored on your device unless you choose to export or share them.

## Data and Privacy

Fintify is designed as a local-first app.

Your portfolio records are stored on your device using local app storage unless you choose to export, share, or otherwise provide them outside the app.

Fintify may request market data for securities you add, such as prices, historical prices, dividends, exchange rates, security metadata, and corporate actions.

Fintify does not require a Fintify account and does not connect to your brokerage account.
